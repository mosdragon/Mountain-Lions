Encouraging The Youth

PHYSICS. We understand that physics isn't for everyone, but surviving through it is a necessity for earning a Diploma. Here at A ADVANCE©, we believe that physics doesn't have to be an overwhelming subject that stops anyone from living their dreams, and we have desgined this site so it is accessible through mobile devices so students can brush up on their physics while they're in a waiting room. We encourage students to try to remember a little bit of the knowledge they learn everywhere they go. It may seem uneccessary and geeky, but a Diploma and an easy life are more than enough incentive to practice what you know wherever you go. Let's ADVANCE.

Send us Feedback

In trying to accomplish our mission, we want to frequently update our site and make it more and more useful for students that want to truly learn the fundamentals of physics, so we would love your feedback. We're open to any comments or questions about our site. Remember, we are here for YOU. We want to see YOU earn A's on every test throughout the year and prove to others that physics really isn't the impossible course it's always rumoured to be.
